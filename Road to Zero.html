Road to Zero definition

The Road to Zero deaths on our roads is a Government led initiative that will reduce deaths on our roads.

Where did the Road to Zero inititive start?

The Road to Zero initiative started in Sweden in 1997.

What the Road to Zero foucuses on

- Infrastructure improvements and speed management

Vehicle safety - How safe your vehicle is Car saftey

- Work-related road safety

- Road user choices - training people on how to be safe on our roads

- System management - How the government monitors what happens on our roads

What does safe driving look like?

Safe driving looks like sticking to the rules and not getting distracted

Facts about the Road to Zero Deaths

- Every death on our roads is a tragedy - Crashes on our roads happen too often. The cost of tragies on our roads is 45 billion dollars

- Other countries like the USA are already showing evidence that their approach about zero deaths on their roads is working

- The road to Zero is the strategy that will make us take responsibility for our actions on our roads

- The government is acting on new technology

- Safe roads = a safer and healthier New Zealand
